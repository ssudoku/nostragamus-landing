Gameplay Challenges

  • A challenge is nothing but a group of matches bunched together on the basis of some rules.
  • Some examples of challenges are:
    • EPL Gameweek 27 Challenge
    • India vs Australia Test Match 3 Challenge
    • Champions League Quarter Finals Challenge
  • Each challenge will run for maximum 1 week.
  • Whenever new challenge is posted, you can view them in the ‘New’ section on the challenge screen of the app.
  • When you complete any match in a challenge, a leaderboard will be generated with the points scored in that match by everybody.
  • Your total score over all the matches played by you in the challenge will give your challenge score.
