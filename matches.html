Gameplay Matches

  • For any live match in any sport, be it cricket, football, tennis, etc, we will put up 5 cards.
  • Each card will contain a question with the relevant stat, for example:
    • Virat Kohli has scored 3 centuries in the last 3 matches. Will he score a century today vs Australia.
    • Zlatan Ibrahimovic has scored 17 goals so far in the premier league season. Will he score today vs Everton?
  • Each question will contain 2 or 3 options
  • To answer, you need to swipe the card left, right or up to indicate your selection.
  • Each card will have points associated with that prediction. The points will be indicated at the top of the card.
  • All questions carry positive and negative points for right and wrong predictions.
  • We will compare your predictions with the actual results of the match and give you a points tally for your predictions for that match.
