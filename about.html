About Us

We started off as a sports news website about a year and a half back, and are now developing a gaming app called Nostragamus.

Unlike other sports-based gaming apps, our app is simple - all you have to do is answer a couple of prediction-based questions for live matches happening in football. We setup short 2-3 day long challenges, based, on EPL gameweeks, or Champions League Rounds. Users need to predict events in these matches. Correct predictions win points, and points accumulated over a challenge decide the winner. Winners will be given money as prizes.

Format

In one sentence, the game is about predictions based on live matches in a format that's extremely easy to understand. All you need to do is select the tournament, play the matches and wait for the results.

Modes of Play

There are two modes of play. You can either play against all players participating in a tournament, or you can create your own private group and play in that - whatever you're comfortable with.

Tournaments Covered

Right now, we're covering top 6 teams in EPL, Barca and Real in La Liga and Bayern Munich in Bundesliga. We're also covering all international cricket matches. In case you want us to cover a new sport, or new team, please drop in a line. That's valuable feedback for us, and we'll try to do it this edition itself.

Rewards

Well, all questions highlight an interesting stat about players or teams, and throwing those stats at your friends will definitely show you in a knowledgeable light :)

Apart from that, we're in the process of installing a wallet system in the game when we go live, and we'll load your wallets with something extra when it comes about.
