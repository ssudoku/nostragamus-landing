Gameplay - How to play

  • When you open the game, you will see a set of challenges ranging across sports.
  • Each challenge will run for 3-4 days and will consist of 5-15 matches. Each challenge will also be classified as free-to-play or paid, and will be characterized by an entry fee and a limit of maximum number of players.
  • When you select a challenge in your sport, you will see a set of matches belonging to it. These are the live matches happening in that challenge.
  • You have to answer predictions in each of the matches. Predictions will concern simple concepts such as runs, wickets, catches, economy rate, etc, in cricket, and goals, saves, passes, tackles, etc, in football, and other similar concepts in other sports.
  • You can use powerups to maximize your points based on the confidence of your predictions. These are tools that help you gain more points on predictions you are confident of, or avoid negative points on predictions you are not confident of.
  • You are given only limited powerups for each challenge you enter. So, use them wisely!
